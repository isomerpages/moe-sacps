<h2>Vision, Mission &amp; Core Values</h2>
<p><strong><em>Vision Statement</em></strong>
</p>
<p>Every SACPS girl a confident and curious learner who serves with commitment,
compassion and cheerfulness</p>
<p><strong><em>Mission Statement</em></strong>
</p>
<p>Nurturing each one in her own way by forming hearts, enlightening minds
and empowering spirits to live in <strong>The Way, The Truth and The Life</strong>
</p>
<p><strong><em>Core Values</em></strong>
</p>
<p><strong><em>Faith</em></strong>: Believe and trust in God</p>
<p><strong><em>Charity</em></strong>: Selflessly reaching out with compassion
and touching lives</p>
<p><strong><em>Humility</em></strong>: Being aware of who we are, recognising
our strengths and staying committed to improvement</p>
<p><strong><em>Forgiveness</em></strong>: Seeking first to understand than
to be understood and being gentle to ourselves to move forward</p>
