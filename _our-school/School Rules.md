<h2>School Rules</h2>
<p>Our school rules describe how a pupil of St Anthony’s Canossian Primary
behaves. Our school believes in positive discipline. Students are to adhere
to school rules at all times while they are in school. To be truly Canossian,
our students must learn to respect for self, school and nation.</p>
<h3>Respect for self</h3>
<p><strong>1.&nbsp; Dress code</strong>
</p>
<p>(1.1) Students are to wear the prescribed school uniform. &nbsp; Modification
to the uniform is not allowed.</p>
<p>(1.2) Students are expected to be attired in white canvas shoes with white
socks covering the ankle.</p>
<p>(1.3) Students are to wear their house T-shirt on PE days. &nbsp;Attire
must be neatly tucked in.</p>
<p>(1.4) Students should be neat and tidy at all times. Hair accessories
must be plain and&nbsp;simple and restricted to blue, black or white only.</p>
<p>(1.5) Students are to ensure that their hair should not be coloured and
fringe should be above eyebrows.</p>
<p>(1.6) Students are expected to tie up their hair once it reaches shoulder
length.</p>
<p>(1.7) Students are to keep finger nails short and clean. Pupils are not
allowed to apply nail polish on their fingernails.</p>
<p><strong>2. Accessories</strong>
</p>
<p>(2.1) Students are to ensure that only one small ear stud is allowed on
each ear.</p>
<p>(2.2) Students are not allowed to put on fanciful, brightly coloured dangling&nbsp;earrings.</p>
<p><strong>3. Daily work, homework and assignment</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>(3.1) Students are to take pride in their work.</p>
<p>(3.2) Students are required to hand in homework and assignments on time
as stated by the teacher.</p>
<p><strong>4. Forgery</strong>
</p>
<p>(4.1) Students must not change any information on official documents or
forging a parent’s, guardian’s signature. Forgery is a serious offence.</p>
<p><strong>5. Punctuality</strong>
</p>
<p>(5.1) Students must be in the assembly area by 7.25 a.m. (5.2) Pupils
must be punctual for all lessons. CCA and other school programmes. 6.&nbsp;Attendance
(6.1) Pupils must attend school regularly. (6.2) Pupils must present a
valid medical certificate or valid document when absent from school.</p>
<p><strong>7.&nbsp;Integrity</strong>
</p>
<p>(7.1) Cheating in examinations is a serious offence. Students must be
honest at all times. Students must not cheat in daily work, examinations
or tests.</p>
<p>Respect for teachers, other adults and fellow schoolmates</p>
<p><strong>8. Behaviour</strong>
</p>
<p>(8.1) Students must be courteous at all times to their teachers, adults
and fellow schoolmates.</p>
<p>(8.2) Students must not bully anyone at all times. Bullying also includes
cyber bullying, emotional and physical bullying.</p>
<p>(8.3) Students must be in their best behaviour at all times.</p>
<p><strong>9. Mobile Phone Policy</strong>
</p>
<p>(9.1) Students must seek permission from the Discipline Mistress before
bringing their mobile phones.</p>
<p>(9.2) Students must adhere to the rules of bringing mobile phones. Failing
to adhere to the rules may result in them facing disciplinary act.</p>
<h3>Respect for Nation</h3>
<p><strong>10. National Anthem and Pledge</strong>
</p>
<p>(10.1) Students who are Singapore citizens must sing the National Anthem
and say the pledge.</p>
<p>(10.2) Students must be respectful in their observations of flag-rising,
flag-lowering and other school ceremonies.</p>
<p>(10.3)&nbsp;Students will take the Pledge with the right fist over the
heart.</p>
<p>(10.4)&nbsp;Students must be in respectful at prayer time.</p>
